BHANOYI SECONDARY SCHOOL
Admission Policy (Summary)

1. Purpose
To provide a fair and transparent process for learner admissions.

2. Eligibility
Placement is subject to available space, grade capacity, and required documentation.

3. Required Documents
- Learner ID/birth certificate
- Latest report
- Parent/guardian ID
- Proof of residence

4. Process
Applications are reviewed in submission order and according to school criteria.

5. Communication
Parents/guardians are informed of outcomes through official channels.

For full policy details, contact the school office.
